Synthetic Encyclopedia Article

The Synthetic Article is a deliberately hand-crafted document used by the mdrcel differential test harness during development. It is not fetched from the live network; it exists so that downstream stages have a stable, byte-identical article-shaped input to exercise extraction against.

Body structure

The page mimics the salient shape of an encyclopedia article: a leading summary paragraph, section headings, and several paragraphs of running prose surrounded by navigation and footer boilerplate that a good extractor should strip. The prose here is intentionally mundane and self-describing so that the expected extracted text is obvious to a human curating a gold entry.

A second body paragraph adds enough tokens that token-set Jaccard and the normalised edit distance produce non-degenerate values when this fixture is scored against an oracle or a future crate output.
